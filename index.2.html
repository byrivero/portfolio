2D/3D Animator and Interactive Multimedia Content Creator

With a background in audiovisual production, image, and 3D animation, I develop projects that combine technique, aesthetics, and narrative. I've worked in video editing, post-production, visual effects, and photography, receiving awards such as Best Short Film and Best Special Effects.

I'm a curious mind, always exploring new ideas and ways to tell visual stories through movement and technology.
